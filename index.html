Kate Bowie is a journalist based in Bristol/Brighton/London. She’s currently on staff as a music, culture and current affair journalist at The Latest Brighton. She is also Co-Deputy Arts Editor at Epigram, Bristol’s award-winning student newspaper. Her work, which encompasses topics from local news to opinion features, has appeared in publications including BN1, The Brighton Indy, Rife Magazine, The Indiependant and Sundial Journal. She also has experience in TV presenting, podcast production and social media management.
